The Days We Lived

Posted at — Mar 3, 2021

A whole year has passed. Who knew I am going to miss my hall room after the lockdown was imposed in March. At that time we were all quite happy as exams got postponed but that happiness did not last more than a week I guess. You miss your wingies, and that friend’s room where you spent most of your time watching movies or just chilling. With most of that gone now, I have nothing in my life, just my laptop and four walls of boredom.

That NesCafe Time

Who wants to eat the LBS mess food, when you have Nescafe around. It was my favorite place to loot my friends (or get looted by them! But I was beware of them!). Classes were boring as hell but when you have some friends to pay for your coffee and burger at Nescafe after the classes, you forget everything and live the moment. Memories. I miss that grilled burger a lot.

The Sweet Torture during Illu

I think I have enough practice to escape even the Alcatraz prison. Well, I learned the art of escaping during Illu (evading the eagle eyes of seniors). But, I am quite sure that some of my friends are extremely good at that. I just don’t want to go into the details of Illu, as I will cry. Just missing those nights for sure.

The Movie Night

Ever binge-watched a movie series with your friends in your hall room? Well, I did. Even Game of Thrones season 8 is bearable with friends.

The GPL

I literally enjoyed this the most. But certainly, my friends did not. I don’t know why!! A slipper must have felt like a sword to them.

Honorable mention to Holi celebration and Ram Dada’s Lassi.
